Industrial Development Corporation Bursary

Helping students with financial need — South Africa

About this Bursary

The IDC External Bursary is open to unemployed eligible South African citizens with financial needs. Applicants should be studying or intend to study full-time towards the below listed qualifications.

Requirements

    NOTE: ALL DOCUMENTS’ CERTIFICATION MUST NOT BE OLDER THAN 3 MONTHS
  • Certified copy of ID
  • Proof of combined household income
  • Proof of all academic results to date (Gr 12 certificate and full tertiary academic record)
  • Recently certified copy of latest pay slip if employed.
  • Both parents 3 months bank statement if employed
  • 6 months’ bank statement if unemployed & self-employed.
  • SASSA Letter stating the grants received if applicable.

What this Bursary Covers

  • Full tuition fees.
  • Accommodation allowance.
  • A laptop ( once-off).
  • Meal allowance
  • Transport allowance

How to Apply

Prepare your documents (ID, academic transcript, proof of income, motivation letter) and click the button below to apply through the official portal.
