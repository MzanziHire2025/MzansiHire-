Faircape Health provides the full continuum of care at six world-class healthcare facilities located on-site at six luxury, retirement villages situated in prime locations across the Western Cape.

We are currently seeking an Assistant Carer Supervisor to join our team at our facility Noordhoek Manor. (This position will suit candidates residing in the Southern Suburbs or Fish Hoek area)

The Assistant Carer Supervisor will ensure that the residents receive the highest standard of care and assist the Carer Supervisor in supporting and guiding the carer team in their day-to-day performance of their jobs.

Requirements:
  • Grade 10 - 12
  • Acknowledged carer qualification (advantageous)
Responsibilities:
  • Supervise and coordinate the carer team by ensuring they are within their allocated areas
  • Ensure the care team is consistently following patient care plans and completing daily charting accurately.
  • Monitor that the carers are following policies, procedures and reporting non-compliance to the Carer Supervisor.
  • Assist carers with care tasks, activities, and exercises when necessary
  • Ensure that the carers respond promptly to call/panic buttons, and oversee that the accurate procedure is followed
Experience and Knowledge :
  • Proven experience within a management/supervisory capacity
  • Experience working as a carer would be an advantage
  • Possess strong and accurate computer and administrative skills to manage documentation and records efficiently
Specific requirements
  • Clear health record
  • Clear criminal record
  • Clear credit record
  • Contactable references
