EASTC, in partnership with SEDFA, is offering this life-changing opportunity in Gauteng, Mpumalanga & Nothern Cape , giving unemployed youth the chance to earn a qualification, gain hands-on training, and step confidently into the world of electrical engineering.

This apprenticeship is more than just training; it is:
  • A gateway to becoming a qualified Electrical Artisan
  • A chance to access employment opportunities in construction, manufacturing, mining, and renewable energy
  • A paid opportunity (R3 000 monthly stipend)
  • A structured, quality-controlled pathway designed by trusted training providers
  • A rare chance for Mpumalanga youth to receive Gauteng-level training
Requirements
  • South African citizen
  • No Age Restrictions
  • Learner must be from mpumalang, Gauteng, or Nothern Cape
  • Grade 12 certificate with Maths and Physical Science will be an added advantage
  • Must be Unemployed and not currently in education or training
  • Willingness to relocate to Gauteng for the duration of the apprenticeship
  • Pass a basic literacy and numeracy test
  • update CV, Certified ID, School Results and Proof of Residence ( No pictures, purely scanned documents)
    • How to Apply
    • Send Your Documents Via Email To projects@eastc.co.za
    • Submit all required documents as specified in the application form
    • Applications close on 04 January 2026
