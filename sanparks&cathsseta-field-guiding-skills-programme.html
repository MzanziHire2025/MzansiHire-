The SANParks & CATHSSETA Field Guiding Skills Programme is a structured skills development initiative aimed at equipping unemployed South African youth with professional field guiding and nature-based tourism skills. The programme combines theoretical training and practical workplace experience within SANParks reserves.

Minimum Requirements:
  • Be a South African citizen
  • Be under the age of 35
  • Have Grade 12 (Matric)

Key learning areas include:
  • Introduction to field guiding principles
  • Wildlife identification (flora & fauna)
  • Conservation ethics and safety
  • Visitor interaction and guiding techniques
  • Environmental awareness
  • Professional conduct in protected areas
