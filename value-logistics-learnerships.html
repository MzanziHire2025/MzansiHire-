Value Logistics is a leading South African logistics and supply chain solutions provider, offering end-to-end services across transport, warehousing, distribution, and contract logistics. The company supports businesses in multiple industries by delivering efficient, reliable, and scalable supply chain solutions tailored to their operational needs. With a strong focus on innovation, operational excellence, and skills development, Value Logistics plays an active role in driving economic growth while creating employment and training opportunities within the logistics sector.

Minimum Requirements
  • Age: Applicants must be between 21 - 29 years old.
  • Education: Matric Certificate with a 50% pass rate.
Recruitment and Selection Process
  • Face-to-face Interview: The interview process is panel-based at the relevant region.
  • Literacy and Numeracy Assessment: You will be required to complete assessments to determine your proficiency.
  • Criminal Check: A background check will be conducted.
Learnership Structure
  • Classroom Training: You will attend theoretical and practical training one week per month.
  • Work Schedule: You will work Monday to Friday from 07:00 to 17:00, with possible overtime as needed.
  • EISA Examination: You will need to write the EISA exam at the end of the programme.
  • Workplace Experience: Workplace experience will be gained in an operational environment.
  • Monthly Stipend: A stipend will be provided for the purpose of travel to the site where the learner will be housed.
