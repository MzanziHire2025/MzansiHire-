Santam Bursary

Helping students with financial need — South Africa

About this Bursary

The Santam Bursary Programme intend to produce a steady stream of skilled, capable and high calibre graduate learners with core technical skills, and behavioural and leadership competencies. This in return makes bursary opportunities available to young talented individuals studying in areas such as actuarial science, agriculture, underwriting, hydraulics and insurance assessment. The initiative was established to ensure Santam builds a talent stream and South Africa has the required skills to grow our economy.

WHO CAN APPLY?

    Students pursuing a Postgraduate Diploma, Bachelor’s degree, Honours degree in:

  • Actuarial, Data Analytics, Data Sciences, Statistics
  • Agriculture
  • Civil, Geomatics, Mechanical Engineering
  • Construction
  • Insurance Technical Accounting, Economics, Finance, Mathematics
  • Risk Management

What this Bursary Covers

    The bursary will cover costs (capped) for the following:
  • Registration/Tuition fee
  • Accommodation (Deposit, admin fees, etc.)
  • Learning material (Books, e-Manuals, etc.)
  • Laptops
  • Monthly allowance
  • Travel/Transport allowance

How to Apply

Prepare your documents (ID, academic transcript, proof of income, motivation letter) and click the button below to apply through the official portal.

Only applications from South African citizens will be considered.
